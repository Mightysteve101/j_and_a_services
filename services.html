  • Whether you're breaking ground on a brand-new structure or breathing fresh life into an existing space, our team delivers exceptional results that align with your vision and budget. Throughout the process, we maintain strict adherence to building codes and regulations while working efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life or business operations.

  • Our licensed electrical contractors provide comprehensive electrical solutions for both residential and commercial properties throughout New Mexico. From basic repairs and upgrades to complete system installations for new construction, our electricians combine technical expertise with a commitment to safety and reliability. We understand that electrical work requires precision and adherence to strict codes and standards, which is why we employ only qualified professionals who stay current with the latest regulations and best practices. Our electrical services include panel upgrades, lighting installation, outlet and switch replacement, troubleshooting and repairs, electrical system design, and specialized solutions for unique needs. We take pride in our attention to detail, ensuring that all electrical work is not only functional and efficient but also neatly installed with minimal disruption to your property.

  • J&A Services delivers exceptional concrete solutions that combine structural integrity with aesthetic appeal for both residential and commercial applications. Our concrete experts handle everything from foundations and flatwork to decorative concrete features that enhance your property's appearance and value. Understanding the unique challenges of New Mexico's climate, we carefully select concrete mixes and reinforcement methods that ensure durability in our region's specific conditions. Our concrete services include foundation pouring and repair, driveways and walkways, patios and pool decks, decorative stamped concrete, concrete countertops, and specialized applications for commercial properties. For each project, we pay careful attention to proper site preparation, precise formwork, and appropriate finishing techniques to create concrete elements that remain beautiful and functional for years to come. Our team's experience with both basic and advanced concrete applications allows us to tackle projects of any complexity with confidence and exceptional results.

  • Our excavation capabilities include both utility and hydro excavation, providing precise ground preparation for construction projects of all sizes. With state-of-the-art equipment and experienced operators, we ensure safe, efficient excavation services that create a solid foundation for your project's success. We understand that proper excavation is critical to the long-term integrity of any construction project, which is why we approach each job with careful planning and precise execution. Our team evaluates soil conditions, considers drainage requirements, and identifies potential challenges before beginning work, ensuring that the excavation process proceeds smoothly and sets the stage for subsequent construction phases. Whether you need site preparation for a new building, utility trenching, grading and leveling, or specialized hydro excavation for sensitive areas, our team has the expertise and equipment to complete the job correctly and efficiently.

  • Maximize your property's energy efficiency with our professional sprayfoam insulation services. This advanced insulation method creates a seamless barrier against heat transfer, reducing energy costs and enhancing comfort throughout your home or commercial building. Unlike traditional insulation materials, sprayfoam expands to fill gaps and create an airtight seal that significantly improves your building's thermal envelope. Our insulation specialists are trained in proper application techniques to ensure optimal coverage and performance, taking care to address all critical areas including attics, walls, crawlspaces, and rim joists. We offer both open-cell and closed-cell foam options, recommending the appropriate type based on your specific needs and budget considerations. Property owners who invest in our sprayfoam insulation services typically report noticeable improvements in comfort, reduced HVAC system workload, and lower energy bills, making this service a valuable investment in your property's long-term efficiency and comfort.

  • Our skilled welders and fabricators create custom metal solutions for projects ranging from structural components to decorative elements. Whether you need repairs to existing metal structures or custom fabrication for new installations, our team delivers precision craftsmanship with attention to both function and form. We work with a variety of metals including steel, aluminum, and stainless steel, selecting the appropriate material and welding technique for each specific application. Our welding services include structural steel fabrication, custom railings and gates, decorative metal elements, equipment repair, and on-site welding for larger installations or repairs. For custom fabrication projects, we work closely with clients to understand their vision and requirements, creating detailed plans before beginning the fabrication process. Our commitment to quality ensures that all welded components not only meet structural requirements but also feature clean, professional finishes that enhance the overall appearance of your project.

  • Our advanced leak detection services help identify and address water loss issues quickly and accurately, saving you money on water bills and preventing potential damage to your pool structure or surrounding property. Using specialized equipment and proven techniques, we locate leaks with minimal disruption, then implement effective repair solutions. We understand that even small leaks can lead to significant problems over time, which is why we approach each detection job with thorough attention to detail. Our technicians employ multiple methods including pressure testing, dye testing, and electronic leak detection to pinpoint the exact location of leaks in your pool's shell, plumbing, or equipment. Once identified, we address the issue with appropriate repair techniques, whether that involves patching, pipe replacement, or equipment repairs. Our comprehensive approach ensures that we not only fix the immediate problem but also identify and address any underlying issues that could lead to future leaks.

  • Enhance your pool area with cool deck surfaces that remain comfortable underfoot even on New Mexico's hottest days. Our cool deck installations provide both practical benefits and aesthetic appeal, creating a safer and more enjoyable pool environment for your family and guests. Traditional concrete pool decking can reach uncomfortable and even dangerous temperatures during summer months, but cool deck technology reflects heat and stays significantly cooler to the touch. Our installation process includes thorough preparation of the existing surface, application of the specialized cool deck material, and careful finishing to create a uniform, attractive appearance. We offer various textures and color options to complement your home's exterior and landscape design while providing the functional benefit of reduced heat absorption. In addition to new installations, we also provide cool deck resurfacing for existing pool decks that need refreshing or repair, extending the life of your pool area while improving both safety and appearance.

  • Keep your pool in pristine condition year-round with our comprehensive maintenance and equipment services. From regular cleaning and chemical balancing to equipment installation and repairs, our experienced technicians ensure your pool remains beautiful, safe, and properly functioning at all times. Our monthly maintenance programs include thorough cleaning, water testing and chemical adjustment, equipment checks, and preventative maintenance to identify potential issues before they become serious problems. For equipment installation and repairs, we work with leading brands of pumps, filters, heaters, automation systems, and salt chlorination equipment, providing expert service that maximizes performance and longevity. Our technicians receive ongoing training on the latest pool technology and maintenance techniques, ensuring that your pool receives care that reflects current industry best practices. Whether you need routine maintenance, seasonal opening and closing services, or major equipment upgrades, our team provides the knowledge and attention to detail that keep your pool operating at its best year after year.
